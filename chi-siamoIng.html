About Us

Our history and our mission

Our Story

Meteo Home Security was born from a school project with the goal of making homes safer and more comfortable through Arduino technology.

The idea is simple yet revolutionary: to create a system that automatically detects temperature, the presence of dangerous gases, and weather conditions, opening and closing windows intelligently.

Our Mission

Security

Protecting families from dangers such as gas leaks through intelligent automation.

Comfort

Always maintaining the perfect climate at home without any effort.

Efficiency

Reducing energy consumption through intelligent ventilation management.
